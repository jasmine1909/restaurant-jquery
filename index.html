About Our Restaurant

Becoming an American restaurateur had been Kind’s dream since childhood. He and business partner Isabelle Roos traveled the United States searching for just the right spot to open a fine dining restaurant, and found it in lovely Spring Hill, Florida.

Since opening in 1999 the restaurant has remained true to it’s vision. Chef Kinds specializes in delicious Belgian cooking such as Coquille St. Jacques, Dover Sole and Steak topped with traditional Belgian sauces.

Wedding Party
